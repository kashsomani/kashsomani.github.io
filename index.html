BIRTH DATA PROJECT

  • Analyzed a dataset containing upwards of 6000 baseball players
  • Observed that August had the most births, and was almost the double of July
  • Upon further research I realized that this was because the birth cut off for most Junior level
    baseball leagues was July 31st, giving people born in August a considerable age advantage
